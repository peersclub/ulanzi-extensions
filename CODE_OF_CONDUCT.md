# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and welcoming. Assume good intent, critique ideas
not people, and keep discussion focused on the project.

Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior isn't tolerated. Maintainers may
remove comments, commits, issues, or contributors that violate this.

Report concerns by opening a confidential issue or contacting the maintainers.

This is a lightweight summary in the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
